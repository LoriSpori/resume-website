Who am I?

I'm an Information Technology student with an interest in the world wide web. My career goal is to be a steady full-stack Web Developer with UI/UX design experience.

Other than that, I'm an life-long artist who likes playing games and listening to a variety of music. I love being creative, especially when it comes to digital drawing. Making websites is a skill I'm hoping to master. I've used technology all my life, so I'm excited about all the new websites I can create or learn about.

What is this website for?

This website was made for educational purposes, but I wanted it to make it personalized. I created all the art used for making this website look appealing, which I found really fun to do. It also gave me a chance to showcase my art in a way I haven't done before. While IT is mostly technical, I like how there is also a way you can be creative. This is actually the first semi-complicated website I created, so I wanted to see the most I can do as a beginner.

Plans for the future

I plan on improving on this website as I continue to gain more technical skills. It was a great step for my future career goal. Anything I create is special to me in it's own way, so I'd love to see this website grow with me. I do admit that I appreciate how this website looks currently, since I have a fondness for older internet aesthetics. Growing up, the websites I used looked similar to this and it brings a smile to my face! I'm finally understanding how they were made.
